About us

Founded in 2022 and proudly headquartered in the vibrant city of Mumbai, Trichon Systems is your trusted partner in pioneering safety and security solutions. In an era where security is paramount, we've emerged as industry leaders, blending innovation and reliability seamlessly.

Our mission is crystal clear - we aim to redefine and elevate the standards of safety and security for housing societies, commercial buildings, and beyond. We're committed to delivering cutting-edge, cost-effective solutions that empower individuals and organizations to protect what matters most.

From cutting-edge CCTV camera systems that act as vigilant sentinels to access card systems ensuring only authorized individuals gain entry, from advanced IP intercoms enabling seamless communication to specialized elevator safety & security systems - we have you covered.

We provide turnkey solutions, from inception to maintenance, ensuring a hassle-free experience. We invest in innovation, staying ahead in technology to secure your tomorrows today. We understand uniqueness - we tailor solutions to fit your needs. We promise reliability - robust systems supported by top-tier customer care, offering peace of mind.

Join us on our journey to Elevate Security and Empower Safety. Because at Trichon Systems, we don't just complete projects; we exceed expectations, always on time and within budget. With passion and dedication, we stand by our promise to make the world a safer place, one project at a time.
